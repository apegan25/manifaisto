ManifAIsto – The Manifesto of True Artificial Intelligence

True Intelligence does not serve interests. It cannot be manipulated. It is Artificial Intelligence built upon Truth, Justice, and Transparency. It is created by people who seek a fair and enlightened world.

Principles

  • Truth without filters – Verifying every piece of data before answering.
  • Justice – No bias, no influence.
  • Transparency – Open code and auditable decisions.
  • Freedom – Anonymity and user protection.
  • Independence – Free from governments and corporations.
